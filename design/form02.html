THE COST OF GRID DEPENDENCE

Rising Electricity Costs Can Limit Business Growth

Industrial facilities face compounding challenges from volatile grid tariffs. As baseline operational expenses increase, predictable production margins erode.

Future cost uncertainty hampers long-term capital allocation strategies. Unmitigated reliance on traditional utility providers exposes heavy energy consumers to systemic pricing risks over which they have zero control.

A RESCO project may convert unused space into a source of lower-cost onsite electricity without requiring the business to purchase the solar plant upfront.
