Frequently Asked Questions about RESCO Solar

Technical and commercial details regarding our Renewable Energy Service Company model.

Who owns the solar plant?

expand_more

Akuntha Projects or our financing partners retain full ownership of the solar installation throughout the duration of the Power Purchase Agreement (PPA). At the end of the term, ownership may be transferred to your facility.

How is payment handled?

expand_more

You pay only for the electricity generated by the system at a pre-agreed tariff rate, which is typically significantly lower than grid rates. You will receive a monthly invoice based on actual generation metered at your site.

What is the minimum consumption required?

expand_more

The RESCO model is optimized for high-energy industrial and commercial facilities. We typically require a minimum monthly consumption profile that supports at least a 500kWp system size to ensure economic viability for both parties.

Are there lease arrangements involved?

expand_more

Yes, a nominal roof or land lease agreement is executed to allow us access and rights to operate the plant on your premises for the duration of the PPA.

Who is responsible for maintenance?

expand_more

Akuntha Projects handles 100% of Operation & Maintenance (O&M). Our engineering teams monitor performance, conduct regular cleaning, and perform preventive and corrective maintenance at zero additional cost to you.
